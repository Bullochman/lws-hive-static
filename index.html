📋 CSV FORMAT GUIDE

Upload a CSV with these exact columns (in any order):

Member  or  Name
Rank  → R5, R4, R3, R2, or R1
HQ Level  or  HQ Lv.
Total Power  → e.g. 54.2M
Notes  (optional)

Extra spaces are trimmed automatically. Duplicate names keep the highest rank row.

💡 Pro tip: Have your leader read out each member's name, rank, HQ level, and power out loud while someone fills in the spreadsheet — takes about 5 minutes for a full alliance.
